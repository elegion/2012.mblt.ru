International conference on mobile technology

Mobile applications in business: benefits, opportunities and prospects

#MBLT12 conference brings together the key players of the mobile technology arena. This conference format is expected to be unusual for an international conference of such scale: dividing the discussion into panels, organizers motivate participants to have a look at cutting-edge mobile developments of leading world companies. MBLT prominent guests will be answering questions of famous hosts. The conference will be held in English and include a number of sections like Device Manufacturers, Social Services, Mobile Publishing, Startups and many others. 

 

The agenda will be of interest for everyone who is involved in mobile application development. Top notch speakers will deliver versatile presentations and lead hands-on workshops for a broad audience.

Organizers:  

Yandex Mobile Camp

Everytiong regarding mobile development

Yandex Mobile Camp with Yandex mobile developers will take place in one of the workshops during the conference.

 

Yandex Mobile Camp is based on free form of communication and discussion of all topics related to mobile development.
